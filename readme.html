About this Project

Discover Bio Bío is a student project developed at DUOC UC, School of Tourism, with contributions from the English Programe. It aims to showcase the cultural and natural richness of the Bío Bío Region through a responsive, accessible, and modern website.

Design Decisions

  • Typography: Outfit, chosen for clarity and modern aesthetics.
  • Colors: Primary #009A96, Secondary #FFB71B, inspired by local identity.
  • Layout: Grid-based cards for destinations, with filters by category and subcategory.
  • Animations: Fade-in reveal for a dynamic but lightweight experience.
